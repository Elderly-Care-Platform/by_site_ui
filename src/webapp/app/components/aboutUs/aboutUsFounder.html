Founder

My name is Vladimir Ruppo, I am 52 years old. Out of them I spent 24 years in St.Petersburg, Russia, where I was born and brought up. I worked for 12 years in Jerusalem, Israel. And for the last 16 years I have been in Bangalore, building and leading big software organizations of 1,000+ developers. So Bangalore has become my home.

Many of my friends were puzzled, when I left my corporate executive job, to establish an elder care startup. But it felt just right for me, as I always wanted to do something of meaning and value – for other people and for myself.

I would like to change the way people are aging in India - and the way they are taken care of.

Hence, BeautifulYears.com.

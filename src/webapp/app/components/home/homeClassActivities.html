Classes & Activities
for Senior Citizens in Bangalore

Old age days can be lonely for elders and loved one's boredom can poses numerous challenges for caregivers. Sometimes finding the answer to this requires a bit of creativity and encouragement from like-minded people.

At BeautifulYears we conduct classes for Senior citizens at our Elder Care Experience Store in Koramangala. We regularly conduct Smartphone classes for elders, Yoga for elders,workshops for Parkinson's and more fun activities for elders.
